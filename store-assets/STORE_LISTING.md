## Full description
**Tracking that gets out of your way.**

Eat Sleep Train Repeat is a minimalist daily health tracker built on one idea: if
logging takes more than a few seconds, you won't do it. So it doesn't.

Type the way you actually talk — "two eggs and toast," "bench 3x5 at 185," "slept 7
hours" — and the app turns it into clean, structured entries. No endless dropdowns, no
barcode hunting, no friction.

**Three things, every day:**
- 🍳 **Eat** — jot what you ate; get calories and protein without the data-entry chore.
- 😴 **Sleep** — log it in a tap and watch the pattern build.
- 🏋️ **Train** — record lifts and cardio, and re-log a repeat session in one tap.

**Repeat** is the point: the more you log, the clearer your patterns become.

**Private by design.**
Your logs live on your device. No account, no sign-up, no ads, no tracking. It works
offline, opens instantly, and keeps your data yours.
