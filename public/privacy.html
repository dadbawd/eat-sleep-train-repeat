Privacy Policy

Eat Sleep Train Repeat · Last updated: June 28, 2026

Eat Sleep Train Repeat ("the app") is a personal health tracker for logging food, sleep, and training. This policy explains exactly what data the app handles. The short version: the app keeps your logs on your own device, has no user accounts, and does not track you.

What we store, and where

  • Your logs stay on your device. Everything you record — food entries, sleep, workouts, and history — is saved locally in your device's browser/app storage. It is not uploaded to us, and we cannot see it.
  • No account, no sign-in. The app does not ask you to create an account or log in, and does not collect your name, email, phone number, contacts, location, or device identifiers.

The one time data leaves your device: AI parsing

The app has an optional feature that turns plain-language notes (for example, "two eggs and toast" or "bench 3x5 at 185") into structured entries. When this feature is used, the text you typed for that entry is sent over a secure connection to our parsing service, which forwards it to our AI provider (Anthropic, the makers of Claude) to interpret it and return the structured result.

  • Only the text of that single entry is sent — nothing else from your logs.
  • We do not store the text on our servers; it is used only to produce the parse and then discarded.
  • Anthropic processes the text to generate the response under their own terms; we do not permit it to be used to train their models.
  • If you are offline or the service is unavailable, the app falls back to on-device parsing, and no data is sent.

No tracking, no ads, no analytics

The app contains no advertising, no analytics or tracking SDKs, and no third-party trackers. We do not build a profile of you or sell or share any data.

Deleting your data

Because your data lives only on your device, you are in full control of it. You can remove all of it at any time by clearing the app's storage in your device settings, or by uninstalling the app. Since we do not keep your logs on our servers, there is nothing stored with us to request deletion of.

Children

The app is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the date at the top of this page.
